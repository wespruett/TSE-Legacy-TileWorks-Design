Custom Design Services

Legacy TileWorks offers both design-only consulting and complete design-to-installation service. Cameron can develop layouts, patterns, accent bands, mosaics, inlays, borders, medallions, feature walls, and other custom details for clients who need a professional plan, or carry the design through preparation and final installation.

  • Design-Only Service: Concept development, layout planning, pattern design, material guidance, trim planning, and installation-ready specifications.
  • Design Through Completion: The full design process plus preparation, waterproofing where required, construction of tile-related features, installation, grout, sealant, and final walkthrough.
